Hi I am Bunny!

Pet

Bunny, the Princess, is 2 years old and is in the prime of her youth. She is a Persian cat (Chinchilla), one of the oldest breeds in the feline world, originating from the Middle East, near the Persian Gulf, specifically Afghanistan and Iran. She is like a "flower of wealth and prosperity" in the human world.

She has a gentle and elegant personality, with a noble and magnificent temperament. She enjoys tranquility and does not easily create a ruckus or make loud noises. She adapts well to new environments, is responsive, and very affectionate. Her appearance is sweet and adorable, appearing calm on the outside but lively on the inside. She is relatively independent but conceals her true emotions. She yearns for her owner's love and is sincere with people, making communication easy. She is highly trainable but tends to be prone to jealousy. She is a proud cat.
